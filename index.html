Graduation thesis

A Study on the Status Quo and Governance of the Digital Divide Among the Elderly

In the context of aging and digitalization, China is facing a new social governance problem - the digital divide of the elderly. How to bridge the digital divide, make the digital dividend cover different age structures, and make the construction of digital government pay attention to each group has become an urgent problem to be solved. This paper uses questionnaires to find the usage and dilemma of the elderly group in high-frequency service projects for the elderly group, such as online payment, intelligent medical treatment, and government services, analyzes the performance of the current digital divide among the elderly from the three levels of access gap, use gap and knowledge gap, and analyzes practical countermeasures according to the phenomenon and the demands of the elderly group,so as to improve the govarnance effect. In the context of the integration of digital government into residents' lives, give play to the government's leadership, guide individuals, families and society to coordinate and jointly cross the digital divide of the elderly.

Keywords：Elderly population; Digital divide governance; Digital government
